The Band

What do we do?


The Monkees are an American rock and pop band featuring Davy Jones, Micky Dolenz, Peter Tork and Michael Nesmith.
Based in Los Angeles, we produce pop rock music with psychedelic notes.
We are active since the 60's and keep playing in festivals and events.

We have an extensive career with TV shows, albums, singles and still new music coming out. Our discography includes The Monkees (1966), More of The Monkees (1967), Headquarters (1967), Pisces, Aquarius, Capricorn & Jones Ltd. (1967)... You can listen to a sample of our music here.
